Who We Are

Home Genie is more than just a service provider - we're a community of professionals dedicated to making your life easier.

Connecting Homes with Trusted Professionals

Founded in 2015, Home Genie began with a simple mission: to transform the way people find and book home services. We saw the frustration people faced when trying to find reliable, high-quality service providers, and we knew there had to be a better way.

Today, we've grown into a nationwide platform connecting homeowners with vetted professionals across dozens of service categories. What sets us apart is our rigorous vetting process, transparent pricing, and commitment to customer satisfaction.

Our team comes from diverse backgrounds - from tech entrepreneurs to service industry veterans - all united by a shared passion for solving real problems for real people.

Our Journey

Key milestones in Home Genie's story

2015 - Foundation

Home Genie was founded by Sarah Johnson in her garage, with just three service professionals on the platform.

2016 - First Funding

Raised $1M in seed funding to expand our team and technology platform.

2017 - 10,000 Services

Celebrated our 10,000th completed service booking with 100% customer satisfaction.

2018 - National Expansion

Expanded operations to cover all major metropolitan areas across the country.

2020 - Pandemic Response

Launched our SafeServe initiative to ensure COVID-safe service delivery during the pandemic.

2022 - 1M Customers

Reached the milestone of serving over 1 million happy customers nationwide.

2023 - Future

Launching new AI-powered features to make service matching even smarter and faster.
